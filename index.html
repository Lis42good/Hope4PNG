Our Mission

"To break the cycle of poverty in Papua New Guinea by providing children with access to quality education, healthcare, and nutrition — empowering them to build brighter futures for themselves, their families, and their communities."

About Us

Hope for Papua New Guinea's Children

Hope4PNG was born from a simple belief: every child in Papua New Guinea, regardless of how remote their village, deserves the chance to learn, grow, and thrive. Since 2010, we've been bringing hope to communities across the nation.

Wantok Spirit

Embracing PNG's community values, we work together as one family to uplift every child.

Local Focus

Our programs span 22 provinces, reaching children in the most remote highlands and islands.

Community Driven

We partner with local churches, schools, and leaders to create sustainable solutions.

Transparency

95% of donations go directly to programs. We're committed to accountability.

Our Programs in PNG

We take a holistic approach to child welfare across Papua New Guinea, addressing education, health, and nutrition to create lasting change in communities.

Education

Providing school supplies, teacher training, and scholarships to children in remote villages across the Highlands and Islands.

5,000+ children in school

Healthcare

Mobile health clinics bringing medical care, vaccinations, and health education to underserved communities.

10,000+ treatments annually

Nutrition

Delivering food-support and nutrition education to children and families facing food insecurity in rural districts.

8,000+ meals distributed monthly

Water & Sanitation

Providing clean water access and hygiene education to remote communities, reducing waterborne diseases in PNG.

50+ water wells installed

Women's Empowerment

Empowering mothers and women with income-generating skills, education, and support networks across communities.

3,000+ women trained

Skills Training

Teaching vocational and technical skills to youth, preparing them for employment and self-sufficiency.

1,500+ youth trained annually
